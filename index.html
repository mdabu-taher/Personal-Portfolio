About Me

I grew up in a small neighbourhood where I spent most afternoons playing outside with friends. One of my favourite childhood memories is helping my father repair old electronics at home. Watching him open radios and small devices sparked my curiosity for how things work.

Over the years, that curiosity developed into an interest in technology. Moving to Sweden and beginning my studies in software development has opened new opportunities. I enjoy exploring new tools, learning step by step, and developing my skills through hands-on projects.
